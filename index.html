About Us

Hey Gunupur!! Finally the wait is over. We are here for you with something very innovative. Girlscript,one of the biggest nonprofit tech organization is here in your city. Girlscript aims at changing lives by imparting tech education to everyone. We want to create awareness about technology and want to impart knowledge to our budding technocrats and aspiring students.

Girlscript is the largest and fastest growing tech community of india. Girlscript mission is to encourage individuals to learn how to code and stand out among the crowd. It also supports girls and encourage them to take part and learn new technology. Girlscript helps you to learn many technologies and makes you expert in specific fields.

Our Goal And Vision

  • To help and train students in the field of technology.
  • Bringing out the best in the budding programmers.
  • To expand our community globally with the vision to help beginners in technology
  • Yes we also support women empowerment and provide 60% seats to women but the opportunity is equal and open to everyone irrespective of gender.
